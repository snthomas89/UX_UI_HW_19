Shelby N Thomas

UX Designer, leaving things a little better than I found them.

Design combines analytics, logic, and artistry - it’s creativity with boundaries. I’m fascinated by the human mind, especially when it comes to interacting with technology. I love to understand what draws us in, what pushes us away, and what keeps us coming back for more.

Although I love to make things aesthetically pleasing, I lean more towards UX than UI. I recently started my design career, currently working on redesigning software development request forms for General Motors.

My Skills

UX Research, Testing, Analysis

I love to define the problem and solution with a current product or for a future one. Analyzing qualitative data and drawing conclusions from interviews, surveys and testing is where I shine.

Visual Mockups & Prototyping

Devising a solution is one thing, but bringing it to life visually is another. UI mockups and prototyping are the bulk of my current role as a designer, and is the most significant way I support my team.

Conveying Concepts

Even outside of the design world, I pride myself on communicating clearly. I enjoy explaining things and am a bit of a nerd for writing emails. I use only the words necessary to get the point across, which is important for keeping interfaces neat and conveying information to users.
